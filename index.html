Lipsti.cc

A research studio building AI experiences to augment human thought and creativity.

Lipsti.cc is a research studio based in the San Francisco Bay Area, focused on building AI experiences to augment human thought and creativity. With a deep commitment to design and user experience, Lipsti.cc aims to bridge the gap between artificial intelligence and human creativity, fostering a future where technology and imagination flourish together.

Lipsti.cc is driven by the belief that AI can serve as a catalyst for creative exploration, rather than a replacement for human ingenuity. By focusing on the design and experience aspects of technology, Lipsti.cc crafts AI solutions that are not only advanced but also deeply integrated with the human process of creation and ideation.

Lipsti.cc specializes in developing tools that empower artists, designers, writers, and thinkers to push the boundaries of their imagination. Reach out if this sounds interesting to you.
