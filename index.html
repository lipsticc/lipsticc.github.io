Lipsti.cc is a product studio and accelerator born in the San Francisco Bay Area, launching worldwide.

We are driven by the belief that AI can serve as a catalyst for creative exploration, rather than a replacement for human ingenuity.

We want to build a future where technology and humans work together. We advocate for organizations to adopt AI as means of technological acceleration and not to fear it. We advocate for effective accelerationism.

Currently, we are interested in building for education and learning. In the future, we plan on extending to puzzle games, utilities and tools, and more.

We are looking for funding and partnerships. If you are aligned with our vision for the future, reach out to aarush@svbrain.xyz. We are particularly keen on angel investments. If you are a builder that wants to launch with us, our accelerator program will be out of beta soon and dreamers are encouraged to apply.
