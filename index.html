TextbookGPT →

TextbookGPT was a collection of AI agents built from scratch (chatbot, summaries, question generator, etc.) for a set of popular high school AP textbooks.

It was based on a fine-tuned LLM and a custom data retrieval pipeline with heuristics tuned for each textbook.

We built the data infrastructure and MLOps from scratch and self-hosted it in our own private server, for experimentation on truly owning our stack.

We sunsetted the product at 1,000+ students and 24,000+ queries/month in ~1.5 months solely from word of mouth.
